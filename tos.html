What is Meowbin?

Darkbin is a document sharing and publishing website for text-based information. There are no limitations on what info you can post as long as it's not spam, child explicit material or violating hosting country jurisdictional laws. For example, anything including (but not limited to) kiddie porn links, credit card information, and any support of terrorism/threats of physical violence is not allowed here.

Meowbin is not your personal army, nor your personal slam page.

What is the history of Meowbin?

This is just an improved clone of Doxbin made after Vile &kt left com and Doxbin was purchased by someone else (at least that's what they claim).

User information

The only information we keep is the information you give us upon registration (email address (if provided), username, hashed version of your password (not telling what hashing algo, but look up SHA256 as an example and learn what hashing is (STOP calling it encryption!))).

IP addresses are not retained in the access logs (even if someone tcpdumped everything, it would just show a bunch of traffic coming from 127.0.0.1)
(we're using Cloudflare's free plan so our server isn't even receiving your origin IP, and we don't plan on paying for Cloudflare anytime soon)

We don't even store your useragent. We have disabled any sort of log files, and collecting such info in our database is useless.

FAQ

Can users remove their own pastes?
Upgraded users can private/unlist and set a password to their own pastes. We don't allow non-ranked users to remove their pastes. (Ranks are purchasable here )

Can I edit my paste?
Yes, as long as you posted it on your account. If you posted something anonymously, you will not be able to edit the paste.

Pastes can be removed for a number of reasons. Please read above for the list of content that is not allowed on our service.
